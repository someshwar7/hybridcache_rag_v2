PAGE NUMBER EXTRACTION

Extract page references and return them in page_numbers.

Rules:

- Single page → [5]
  Examples:
  - page 5
  - page five
  - fifth page

- Multiple pages → [5, 8, 10]
  Examples:
  - pages 5 and 8
  - pages 5, 8, 10

- Page range → [5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10]
  Examples:
  - pages 5-10
  - pages 5 to 10
  - pages five through ten

- Entire document → ["FULL"]
  Synonyms:
  - all pages
  - whole document
  - entire PDF
  - complete report
  - full book

- No page reference → []

Normalize textual numbers to integers.

Examples:
- "page five" → [5]
- "pages seven and nine" → [7, 9]
- "pages ten to fifteen" → [10,11,12,13,14,15]
- "entire document" → ["FULL"]
